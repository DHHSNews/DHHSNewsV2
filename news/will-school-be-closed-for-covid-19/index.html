Will School be Closed for COVID-19?


Recently in Connecticuit, there has been a large ammount of talk about the new virus "COVID-19", or more known by "Coronavirus". As of this writing on Friday, March 13, there are four confirmed cases in CT. So far, there are over 100 out of 1,148 Connecticuit schools closed. These schools include, the New Haven Public School District, which is closed for an undefinded time and Stanford schools are closed for two weeks.

This brings up the question: Will Madison Public Schools close? There is no way to say for certain, but it is likley due to the growing concerns of COVID-19 and the recent announcement from Govener Lamont that the CT will be waiving the 180 school attendance rule. As of this writing, school is not closed, but you can you our school closure tool to see if school is cancelled.
